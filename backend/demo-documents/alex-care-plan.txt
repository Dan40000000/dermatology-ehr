Psoriasis and Cryotherapy Care Plan

Visit summary:
Two actinic keratoses on the left temple were treated with liquid nitrogen. Plaque psoriasis is improved on the current plan.

Instructions:
- Keep treated areas clean. A small blister or scab can be normal after cryotherapy.
- Use petrolatum if the areas become dry or irritated.
- Continue methotrexate 15 mg once weekly and folic acid as directed.
- Complete routine monitoring labs before the next psoriasis follow-up.
- Use broad-spectrum SPF 30 or higher every morning.

Call the office for spreading redness, pus, fever, severe pain, or rapidly changing skin spots.
