Biopsy Aftercare and Pathology Follow-up

Visit summary:
A shave biopsy was performed on an irritated chest lesion. Hand eczema treatment was adjusted, and patch testing was discussed.

Instructions:
- Keep the biopsy site covered with petrolatum and a bandage until healed.
- Wash gently with soap and water once daily.
- Avoid latex exposure when possible.
- Use triamcinolone ointment as prescribed for flares.
- Pathology results will be released to the portal when finalized.

Call the office for spreading redness, warmth, pus, fever, or increasing pain.
