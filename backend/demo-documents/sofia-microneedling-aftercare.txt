Microneedling Post-Procedure Instructions

Visit summary:
Microneedling was performed for acne scarring. Hormonal acne treatment with spironolactone and tretinoin was reviewed.

Aftercare:
- Expect redness and mild swelling for 24 to 72 hours.
- Avoid exfoliants, retinoids, and active acids until skin has recovered.
- Use gentle cleanser, bland moisturizer, and strict sunscreen.
- Avoid heavy exercise, saunas, and direct sun for 24 hours.
- Resume acne medications only as directed.
