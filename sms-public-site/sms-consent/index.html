SMS Program Disclosure

Perry Software LLC Text Messaging Consent

Patients may opt in to receive text messages from Perry Software LLC about appointments, check-in links, billing/payment notifications, account alerts, prescription coordination, and support updates related to dermatology care and account workflows. Consent is optional and is not required for treatment, payment, appointment scheduling, portal registration, or any other office service.

How patients opt in

Patients may opt in by checking an SMS consent box during intake, portal registration, patient check-in, or staff-assisted registration, or by texting START to the practice messaging number.

Message types

Appointment reminders, check-in links, billing/payment notifications, account alerts, prescription coordination, and support updates.

Frequency and fees

Message frequency varies by patient activity. Message and data rates may apply.
