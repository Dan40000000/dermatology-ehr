SMS Program Disclosure

Nuvora Health Text Messaging Consent

Patients may opt in to receive text messages from Nuvora Health, operated by Perry Software LLC, about appointments, billing, prescriptions, and care coordination. SMS consent is optional and is not required for treatment, payment, appointment scheduling, portal registration, or any other office service.

How patients opt in

Patients may opt in by checking an optional SMS consent box during intake, portal registration, or staff-assisted registration, or by texting START to the practice messaging number. They can complete registration and care workflows without checking the SMS box.

Message types

Appointment reminders, scheduling updates, billing notices, prescription coordination, and care follow-up messages.

Frequency and fees

Message frequency varies by patient activity. Message and data rates may apply.

Help and opt-out

Reply HELP for help and STOP to opt out at any time. Support: +1 (980) 737-1319.

Consent language used in the product
