Perry Software LLC SMS Opt-In Flow Evidence

This public page documents the SMS consent language and opt-in locations used by the Perry Software LLC dermatology patient-notification program so messaging reviewers can verify the call to action without logging into the patient portal.

Where consent is captured

1. Patient portal profile preferences

Authenticated patients can review their mobile number and enable appointment reminders from the portal preferences screen. The SMS consent disclosure is displayed next to the reminder toggle before SMS reminders are enabled.

2. Patient check-in workflow

During electronic check-in, the patient reviews contact information, including the mobile number used for appointment reminders and check-in links. SMS consent language is presented before consent is captured.

3. Intake form and staff-assisted registration

New or updated intake workflows allow staff or patients to record SMS consent after the patient provides a mobile phone number and sees the same disclosure below.

Exact disclosure shown at consent

By providing your mobile number, you agree to receive text messages from Perry Software LLC at +1 980-737-1319 for appointment reminders, check-in links, billing/payment notifications, account alerts, prescription coordination, and support updates related to your dermatology care and account. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out and HELP for help. Consent is not a condition of purchase. Terms and Conditions: /sms-terms/. Privacy Policy: /sms-privacy/.
