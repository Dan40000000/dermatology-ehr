Dermatology DEMO Office SMS Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Dermatology DEMO Office handles mobile numbers and text-message related information.

Information collected

  • Mobile phone number provided by the patient or authorized representative.
  • Consent status, opt-in date, opt-out date, and message delivery activity.
  • Limited message content needed to route scheduling, billing, prescription, and care-related requests.

How information is used

  • To send appointment reminders, schedule changes, billing updates, prescription coordination, and care follow-up messages.
  • To process HELP, STOP, START, and other consent-management requests.
  • To maintain audit logs for compliance and patient communication history.

Sharing and protection

Mobile numbers and SMS consent records are used only for practice operations and are not sold to third parties. Information may be shared with service providers that support messaging delivery or healthcare operations, subject to applicable privacy and security safeguards.

Opt-out rights

Patients can opt out at any time by replying STOP. They can request help by replying HELP.
